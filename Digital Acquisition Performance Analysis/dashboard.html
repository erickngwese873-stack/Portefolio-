Recommendation: The model suggests a marginal rebalance: increase Facebook (+0.29%) and Website (+0.08%) allocation while slightly reducing Google Ads (−0.15%) and Email (−0.12%). On a $2.5B budget, even these small deltas represent ~$3.7M in reallocation. The tight spread across channels (16.6–16.8%) means no single channel is dramatically underperforming — the primary optimization lever is at the campaign type and audience level, not channel level.
